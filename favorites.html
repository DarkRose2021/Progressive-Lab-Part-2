About The Websites

I use spotify a lot since I listen to music while I work on things or when I go to school. Listening to music helps me focus since I can easily block other things out and since I listen to the same playlist, I can also block out the music. Shinedown is the main band I listen to. I grew up listening to them and some other older rock bands. I've been to a few Shinedown concerts, but that's all. I have a hard time expanding my music because of some of my family, so I just stick to what I grew up on.

My web teacher was learning with us so she didn't know a whole lot about what she was teaching, but I always enjoyed showing her new things that I found and figured out how they worked. I loved that class and I'm sure that she's the reason that I enjoy making websites.
